Notes (May.18.2009)

  • The email box is the only reliable attribute of the member.
  • Both dialogs appear on the public home page of LP.

Registration

  • LP visitor enters 'Email' and 'Username'.
  • LP sends a welcome message to visitor containing a password.
  • LP visitor enters 'Username' and 'Password' and initiates LP.Member.
  • LP member is able to change password.

Forgotten password

  • LP member enters 'Username' and 'Email address'. LP validates email address.
  • LP sends member password to known email address.
  • LP member enters LP with username and password.
